Antoine Bernard-Jean Marfan
French paediatrician, born 23rd June, 1858, Castelnaudary, département Aude, Languedoc-Roussillon région, died 1942.

Marfan's syndrome = A disorder of connective tissue affecting primarily the musculoskeletal system, the cardiovascular system and the eye with dislocation of the lens.
